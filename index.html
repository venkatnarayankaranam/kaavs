Welcome to Our Project

Scene understanding is a crucial area in computer vision and artificial intelligence that focuses on interpreting and analyzing the contents of an image or video. It enables machines to identify objects, segment regions, recognize actions, and infer contextual meaning from a given scene. This technology is widely used in autonomous vehicles, robotics, augmented reality, and assistive technologies.
